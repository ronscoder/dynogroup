DYNAMIC GROUP OF FOUNDATIONS (DGF)

KHURAI THOUDAM LEIKAI, IMPHAL EAST DISTRICT, MANIPUR

ANNUAL REPORT (2013 - 2014)

  1. Free Health Care Camp
    DGF organized “One Day Free Health Care Camp” at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur on 9th Apr. 2013 in co-ordination with local youth club and Health Deptt. 55 persons including 20 women and children had undergone check-up under the programme.
  2. Adult Non-formal Education Programme
    DGF organized “2 Months Adult Non-Formal Education Programme” for giving education to elderly persons from 3rd May to 30th June 2013 at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur. 25 elderly persons including women were given education by promoting reading and writing skills.
  3. Aged Welfare Programme
    DGF organized “One day Aged Welfare Programme” at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur on 29th July 2013 in co-ordination with local youth club. Resource person from Department Of Social Welfare delivered talk on that programme. Clothes, nutritional food and medicines were distributed to 30 aged persons.
  4. Skill Development Programme
    DGF organized “One Day Skill Development Programme” at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East district, Manipur on 4th Aug 2013. The main objective of the programme was to develop skills through proper training among women so that they can improve more on income generation. About 35 women took part on that programme.
  5. Food Processing Training
    As a part of setting up of income generating programme to improve the socio-economic status of rural poor backward women through skill up gradation and creation of self-employment, DGF organized “Three Months Food Processing Training Programme” from 25th Aug to 3rd Dec 2013 at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur. About 30 women were trained in the training programme.
  6. Women and Child Welfare Programme
    DGF organised “One Day Women and Child Welfare Programme” at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur on 24th December 2014 in coordination with local youth club.Resourse persons from Department of Social Welfare delivered talk on that programme Cloths, nutritional foods and medicines were distributed to 20 women and children.
  7. Library Awareness Programme
    DGF has opened a Library cum Information centre at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur with a view to get certain knowledge and aware of Library and Information among the students and general public. It also organized “One Day Library Awareness Programme” on 14th January, 2014.

ANNUAL REPORT (2014 - 2015)

  1. Youth Leadership Camp
    DGF organized “One Day Youth Leadership Camp” at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur on 3rd Apr. 2014 in co-ordination with local youth club. The main objective of the programme was to give awareness among the local youth of the present scenario and how to maintain and procure the quality of leadership among themselves. About 35 youths took part in that programme.
  2. Free Medical Camp
    DGF organized “One Day Free Medical Camp” at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur on 9th May 2014 in co-ordination with local Youth Club and Health Department. 45 persons including 24 women and children underwent Health check-up under the programme.
  3. Sports Development Programme
    DGF has been participating in sports since its inception; it also put effort to develop Games and Sports by conducting tournaments like football, cricket, volleyball and many other games in co-ordination with local Youth Clubs. It has organised 8 tournaments so far.
  4. Solid Waste Management Programme
    DGF organized “One Day Solid Waste Management Programme” at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East district, Manipur on 11th June 2014. The main objective of the programme was to give knowledge of proper disposal of discarded solid waste which in turn can create unsanitary conditions and leads to outbreak of vector-borne diseases spread by rodents and insects due to improper disposal. Resource persons from Department of Bio-resource Management delivered talk on that programme. 35 persons took part on that programme.
  5. Computer Training Programme
    DGF organized “3 months Computer Training Programme” for youth at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur from 1st July to 30th Sept 2014. The main objective of the programme was to impart Computer skills among educated un-employed youths. 30 youths were given training under this programme.
  6. Legal Awareness Camp
    DGF conducted “One Day Legal Awareness Camp for General Public” on 18th October 2014 at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East, Manipur. The main objective of the programme was to make aware about Human Rights and Legal Rights.
  7. Library Awareness Programme
    DGF renovated the Library cum Information centre, Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East Manipur with the view to gain certain knowledge and aware of Library information among the students and general public. It also organised “One Day Library Awareness Programme” on 4th November 2014.
  8. Environment Awareness Programme
    DGF organized “One Day Environment Awareness Programme” at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East, Manipur on 22nd December 2014 with a view to create awareness among the rural masses to keep ecological balance due to deforestation. More than 34 persons took part on that Programme.

ANNUAL REPORT (2015 - 2016)

  1. Vocational Training Programme:
    As a part of setting up of income generating programme to improve the socio-economic status of rural poor backward women through skill up gradation and creation of self-employment, DGF organized “Three Months Vocational Training Programme on Cane and Bamboo” from 11th April to 10th July 2015 at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur. About 35 trainees were trained in the training programme. The trainees were also provided with raw materials and other necessary equipment during the programme free of cost. After the completion of the programme, the beneficiaries were formed into Self Help Group for starting their own production unit as follow up programme.
  2. Agriculture & Horticulture Development Programme:
    DGF organized “One Day Agriculture and Horticulture Development Programme” at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur on 24th July 2015. The main objective of the programme was to impart knowledge on increasing yield of agricultural products and horticultural products and also on prevention of different plant diseases. Some pesticides were also distributed to 33 farmers. Agricultural and Horticultural experts delivered talk on that programme.
  3. Free Medical Camp:
    DGF organized “One Day Free Medical Camp” at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur on 5th Aug 2015 in co-ordination with local youth club and Health Department. 39 persons including 24 women and children underwent Health check-up under the programme.
  4. Youth Awareness Programme:
    DGF organized “One Day Youth Awareness Programme” at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East district, Manipur on 24th Sept 2015 in co-ordination with local Youth Club. The main objective of the programme was to give awareness among the local youth of the present scenario and how to prevent and stay away from drugs and also how to maintain the quality of leadership among them. About 35 youths took part on that programme.
  5. Social Forestry Programme
    DGF conducted “One Day Social Forestry Programme” at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur on 19th October 2015 with a view to create awareness among the rural masses to maintain ecological balance due to deforestation. More than 30 persons took part on that programme. 100 trees were planted along the roadside.
  6. Sports Development Programme
    DGF has been participating in sports since its inception, it also put an effort to develop Games and Sports by conducting tournaments like Football, Cricket, Volley Ball and many other games in co-ordination with local Youth Clubs. DGF has organized 8 tournaments so far.
  7. Self Help Groups Awareness Programmes
    DGF organized “One Day Awareness Programme for SHGs” on 11th November 2015. The main objective of the programme was to assist each SHGs financially for income generation through skill up gradation.
  8. Art and Culture Development Programme
    DGF organized “One Day Art and Culture Development Programme” on 7th December 2015 at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East, Manipur. The main objective of the Programme was to enhance Art and Cultural Dimensions and culturally relevant strategies where cultural values drive a society’s performance and actions such as beliefs, practices, symbols, specific norms and personal values. About 30 persons took part on that programme. Resource persons from Art and Culture delivered talk on that Programme.
  9. Library cum Information centre
    DGF renovated the Library cum Information centre at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur with a view to gain certain knowledge and awareness of Library and information among the students and general public.
  10. Foundation Day Celebration
    DGF organized its Foundation Day on 12th February 2016 at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Imphal East District, Manipur. Many Cultural Programmes were shown on that day and a grand feast for more than 100 people.
