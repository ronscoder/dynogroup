Organizational Profile

Dynamic Group of Foundations (DGF)

The Dynamic Group of Foundations is a state level trust under the Indian Trust Act 1882 and Indian Registration Act 1908 bearing the Registration Number 27 of 2013 with Head Office located at Khurai Thoudam Leikai, Lamlong, Imphal East, Manipur- 795010. The trust is also registered under Section 12(A) and 80(G) of Income Tax Act, 1961, Government of India.

To address above mentioned issues, DYNAMIC GROUP OF FOUNDATIONS focuses on three objectives, namely – Education, Health and Economic Development.

Dynamic Group of Foundations (DGF) works for the development of the community as a whole. However, the needy target groups of the projects of DGF are as follows:

Our Values

Dynamic Group of Foundations(DGF) is committed to providing sustainable development for the underprivileged and vulnerable members of the population with special focus on children and persons living in difficult circumstances by acting as a facilitator through community development providing education, vocational training, health awareness and service, shelter, nutrition counselling, recreation, rescue and rehabilitation, repatriation awareness on rights, emergency relief, capacity building, fundraising, socio-economic, cultural and spiritual development regardless of race, sex, religion or politics to achieve goals. DGF is committed to social justice and work for preservation of human rights. DGF follows the rights based approach with people participation to evolve and transform to a prosperous future. We thus follow the People Empowerment model of ministry. DGF acknowledge and nurture the potentials of all children. We are accountable and transparent to all donors and sponsors.

Our Objectives

The given five domains of evolution, described below, interact to promote sustainable transformation in social, biophysical, political, economic, spiritual and ecclesiastical systems. The five domains of evolution are:

  1. Well-being of children and families

    2. The Project Goal ensures that the capacities of families and communities for survival and growth will be enhanced through championing of their human rights towards health and basic education, as well as spiritual and emotional nurture. Through Economic Development Project, the program have immense scope for the development of children’s and families’ well-being that further seek opportunity to develop a sustainable household livelihood with just distribution of resources, capacity to earn a future livelihood, protection from abuse and exploitation, as well as reduction of the risk of exposure to disasters and HIV/AIDS.

  2. Well-being of children and families

    3. Helping the needy children is the core model of development and evolution. The DGF places children as the centre stage of change and evolution. Children will be participating in the development process in age-appropriate ways throughout the life of Organization; consequently the children will become agents of transformation in their families and communities.

  3. Transformed relationships

    4. The Integration of Education, Health & Economic Development Projects creates an environment to interact, dialogue & build relationship to some degree with the community. Having this opportunity DGF has scope to address to the need of restoring relationships with each other, the environment, and with God. Establishing equitable, just, peaceful, productive and inclusive relationships within households and communities could impact social, political, economic, spiritual and ecclesiastical aspects of community life. The Economic Development Project will directly deal with the issues of unstable ecosystem and will help the community to manage environmental resources.

  4. Interdependent and empowered communities

    5. Projects of DGF are road mapped with the success in mind that can be achieved through the strength of collaboration, coordination, participation & networking. The success of empowerment model can only be ensured through coalition & networking at local, national, regional & global level on mutual respect, transparency and ethical/morals responsibility. The road map ensure that DGF will move into building a culture of people participation that will empower & sustain families and whole communities to influence and shape their growth and transformation meaningfully.

  5. Transformed systems and structures

    6. DGF also captures the issue of systems & structure of the present target community. Building & Strengthening community based organisation for leadership role is the top priority of DGF. The community will be sensitized and made aware of their rights so that just systems and structures would be in the right place and consequently, the community will have affordable & accessible services & resources for themselves.

    Ultimate Goal

    The ultimate goal of DGF in India is that “Community would be able to manage development program, to have ownership in it, and to take a lead in the betterment of future of the community with the available funds and resources.”
